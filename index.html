Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application process? +

Our application process is simple: 1) Download and complete our PDF application form, 2) Provide employment verification and references, 3) Email completed application to us, 4) Application review (24-48 hours), 5) Lease signing and key handover. We require first and last month's rent upfront.

Are pets allowed? +

Yes, some pets are allowed. There's a limit of 2 pets per unit.

What utilities are included? +

None

Is parking available? +

Each unit comes with its own parking spot in the house's driveway. Street parking is also available'

Can I view the unit before applying? +

Absolutely! We encourage viewings and can arrange them within 24 hours of your request. Viewings are available 7 days a week, and we can accommodate evening appointments for working professionals.

What about guest parking and visitors? +

Guests can park on the street at no charge.
