Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application process? +

Our application process is simple: 1) Fill out our online application form, 2) Provide employment verification and references, 3) Credit check ($50 fee), 4) Application review (24-48 hours), 5) Lease signing and key handover. We require first and last month's rent upfront.

Are pets allowed? +

Yes, pet cats are allowed. There's a limit of 2 pets per unit.

What utilities are included? +

None

Is parking available? +

Each unit comes with its own parking spot in the house's driveway. Street parking is also available'

How quickly are maintenance requests handled? +

Non-emergency maintenance requests are typically handled within 24-48 hours. Emergency issues (no heat, plumbing problems, security concerns) are addressed immediately via our 24/7 emergency line. We pride ourselves on responsive property management.

Can I view the unit before applying? +

Absolutely! We encourage viewings and can arrange them within 24 hours of your request. Viewings are available 7 days a week, and we can accommodate evening appointments for working professionals.

What about guest parking and visitors? +

Guests can park on the street at no charge.
